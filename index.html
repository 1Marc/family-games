Avalon Siblings

Dr. Noxious has poisoned Avalon!

Join Charlie and Kiera as they travel across the land to stop the evil Dr. Noxious. As players journey through a vast and enchanting world, they'll navigate varied platformer levels, engage in epic boss battles, and uncover the hidden secrets of Avalon.

Features

  • Immersive Storytelling Cutscenes: Handcrafted cutscenes will take players through the land of Avalon.
  • Play as Fluttering Kiera: Kiera flies over Misty Lake with her trusty laser shooting parrot.
  • Big Scary Bosses: Dr. Noxious and other bosses stand in the way of Kiera and Charlie on their adventure.

Details

  • Genres: Single-player, platformer, story-driven
  • Platforms: Android Play Store, Apple App Store
  • Release Date: TBA
  • Supported Languages: English
